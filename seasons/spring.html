Spring in Montana

Spring is a beautiful time in Montana. Elk and deer are dropping their sheds, the fish are spawning and the snow is melting. The grass is turning green, the flowers are blooming, and the air is warming up. There's something for everyone to do during the Spring time in Montana. Some popular activites include:

  • Fishing
  • Shed hunting
  • Horseback riding
  • Spring skiing
  • Flying a kite
  • Biking

It might be windy outside, but get out and enjoy the fresh air! You'll need it after being inside all winter.
