Summer in Montana

Summer in Montana is know for being hot and dry. Wildfires always rip through Montana during the summers, but there's still plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities. Try doing some of these activities:

  • Hiking
  • Fishing
  • Backpacking
  • Boating
  • Camping
  • Kayaking or canoeing
  • Golfing
  • Watching a sunset

In order to avoid the hot and miserable parts of the day, try doing these activities early in the morning or in the evening.
